Student Chapter

During my time at KTH I have been very active in the student chapter IN-Sektionen. Some of the positions I have held are:

  • Head of the Business Committee
  • Boardmember with responsibility for JML/Equality work
  • Treasurer of Student Pub Association TMEIT
  • Team leader for work team in Student Pub Association TMEIT, 2 semesters
  • Boardmember of the organization group of the yearly Reception, held to welcome new students for the autumn semester.
  • Head organizer of an improv acting group in the Reception

These roles have all provided me with ample experience in project organization, group communication and planning, and staying structured.
