Miscellaneous

Student Chapter

During my time at KTH I have been very active in the student chapter IN-Sektionen. Some of the positions I have held are:

  • Head of the Business Committee
  • Boardmember with responsibility for JML/Equality work
  • Treasurer of Student Pub Association TMEIT
  • Team leader for work team in Student Pub Association TMEIT, 2 semesters
  • Boardmember of the organization group of the yearly Reception, held to welcome new students for the autumn semester.
  • Head organizer of an improv acting group in the Reception

These roles have all provided me with ample experience in project organization, group communication and planning, and staying structured.

Conventions

In my teens I volunteered for many conventions for east asian pop-culture. This involved positions such as website translator, head of cleaning crew, café businesses, and ticket sales.
I also arranged a convention with a group of local teenagers in my hometown, which we grew from 80 attendees to over 250 in three years. The organizational work required applications for fundings and grants, booking premises, booking bands, inviting and accomodating for guest speakers from all over the world, logotype designing, promotional visits to other conventions, ordering merchendise, handling the economics, holding meetings, and much much more. Apart from being a fun activity to partake in, it taught me much in terms of organization and planning. I developed skills in problem solving and thinking on my feet, overcoming obstacles, and communicating with other organizers, attendees, and invited guests.
