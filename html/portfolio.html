As our project in the course Physical Interaction Design and Realization, we created BubblePlay, a device which produces bubbles by spinning a pole. The device used 3D printed gears for the bubble machine and an Arduino board for controlling LED light strips surrounding the device. Click here to download the BubblePlay project report.

2 / 5

In the course Advanced Project course in Interactive Media Technology, we developed the "Move it Move it Microwave", a dance-controlled microwave designed to encourage playful interactions in the kitchen environment. The device used an Arduino board and motion detection software to control the switch in the microwave, producing a microwave which only worked when you danced in front of it. In addition, it plays a musical beat if more than one person participates, which invites for social interactions. Click here to download the "Move it Move it Microwave" project report.
