Academic Projects

Master's Degree KTH

Masters degree in Interactive Media Technology, where I attend the Physical Interaction track. My thesis will be finished in the summer of 2022. A selection of some of the courses and projects I have done during my time at my master's programme:
As our project in the course Physical Interaction Design and Realization, we created BubblePlay, a device which produces bubbles by spinning a pole. The device used 3D printed gears for the bubble machine and an Arduino board for controlling LED light strips surrounding the device. Click here to download the BubblePlay project report.

In the course Advanced Projectcourse in Interactive Media Technology, we developed the "Move it Move it Microwave", a dance-controlled microwave designed to encourage playful interactions in the kitchen environment. The device used an Arduino board and motion detection software to control the switch in the microwave, producing a microwave which only worked when you danced in front of it. In addition, it plays a musical beat if more than one person participates, which invites for social interactions. Click here to download the "Move it Move it Microwave" project report.
