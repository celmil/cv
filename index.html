About Me

My name is Celine. I am 28, I live in Stockholm, Sweden, and I fold my laundry and put it back in the wardrobe straight out the dryer.
I empty my suitcase as soon as I return from a trip, I watch hour-long video essays on things I have never heard about, and I have approximately 80 creative projects going at all times.
With compassion, intelligence, and sense of humor I take pride in being a team player.
I am a Jill of all trades and a devout generalist in constant search for the next topic to nerd out on.
With my trusty weekly planner by my side I am an unstoppable force, and when the to-do list is emptied for the day I am probably knitting, painting, lifting weights, or on a long walk.
